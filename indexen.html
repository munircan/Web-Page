ABOUT US

ADA, the initials of three senior students of the Linguistics Department of the Faculty of Science and Letters, Istanbul University, was incorporated in 1991. The only services rendered by ADA are translation and related consultancy services. Our aim is to offer solutions to the translation problems encountered by most of the companies requiring high quality translation services.

ADA is currently rendering translation services in two offices, one at the Asian side of Istanbul and the other at the European side. Throughout Turkey, from Istanbul to Kilis, and from USA to Malta worldwide, ADA has hundreds of customers.

Our customer portfolio includes international companies, construction companies, medical firms, advertising agencies, financial institutions, shipping and maritime companies, law firms and IT firms.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

Our success is embedded in meeting deadlines and delivering high quality work. We offer you the latest technology and a sophisticated project management system. We are committed to provide accurate, express and excellent service to our customers. Our translators hold advanced academic degrees, and we carefully match their technical and academic credentials to your specific needs. Our translators are technically qualified native speakers providing translations precise, authentic and exactly comparable to the original, including terminology.
